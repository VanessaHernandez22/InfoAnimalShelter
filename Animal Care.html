How to Care For a Pet

Pet Dogs

Depending on the dog breed you can feed it:

• Pure kibble diet
• Or you can mix their diet up with some cooked or raw meat, fish, vegetables and rice.
• Needs a Fresh bowl of water every day.
Routine vet care for a dog:

• Should recieve vaccines, spay/Neuter when young.
• If young healthy dog(2-5 years old), should go to the vet once every year.
• Needs two annual vet check up if an older dog(6-17 years old).
Positive Bonding:

• Reward and train your dog to behave (for example give a treat when they follow an order).
• Petting gently and making your dog feel safe is important.
• Be playful and make sure they get enough exercise (Either by going outside in the back yard or take them on a walk).

Pet Birds

Feeding Birds:

• Grains, sunflower seeds, peanuts, cooked pasta, rice ect (ask pet shops what to feed your specific bird species).
Bird Vet Care:

• Take your bird to the vet once a year, bigger cage is always better for the birds state of mind.
Bird Bonding:

• Talk gently to the bird, make them feel safe, don't move too much around them, keep your voice low, give them their favorite food.

Pet Cats

Cat Feeding:

• Canned cat food, meat or dry cat food should be fed. Cat should be fed on a schedule throughout the day, or they will end up over eating.
• Fresh water every day
Vet care for Cats:

• Adult cats should see the vet once a year.
Bonding with Cats:

• Give them space, let them come to you, and pet them.
• Brush your cat
• Play with your cat, get some toys.
